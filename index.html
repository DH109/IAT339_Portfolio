About Me

I am a third-year design concentration student in interactive arts and technology at Simon Fraser University with an interest in graphic design & 3D modeling.Through the years of studying concepts of design, I have established knowledge in creating immersive design with meaningful interactions to users. I began the design process by creating initial sketches to envisage concepts, to assimilate feedback, and improve it into a final work of art. I have also developed technical skills in using Procreate, Photoshop, Indesign, Onshape, and Figma to assist my design process.
